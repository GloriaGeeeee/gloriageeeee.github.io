IMAGES NEEDED FOR YOUR PORTFOLIO
================================

Please add the following images to this folder:

1. profile-photo.jpg (or .png)
   - Your profile photo
   - Recommended size: 600x800 pixels or similar portrait ratio
   - The photo will automatically be converted to black & white
   - It should show you from about chest level up

2. pepperstone-logo.svg (or .png)
   - Pepperstone company logo
   - Recommended size: 200x50 pixels
   - Transparent background preferred

3. (Optional) You can also add:
   - okx-logo.svg - OKX company logo
   - ibm-logo.svg - IBM iX company logo
   - Any mockup images you want to use instead of the CSS-generated ones

NOTES:
------
- The website will work without these images (it has fallbacks)
- Make sure image filenames match exactly (case-sensitive)
- SVG format is preferred for logos (scalable and crisp)
- JPG or PNG works great for photos

Once you add the images, they'll automatically appear on your website!
